## E-Ink Module

**“Heltec E-Ink Module” product family already included follow options:**

- 7 pin 2.54 header;
- 4 wire SPI bus;
- Power on auto reset circuit;
- On board LDO and boost circuit;
- 3.3 ~ 7V input;
- 0 ~ 50 ℃ operating temperature.
